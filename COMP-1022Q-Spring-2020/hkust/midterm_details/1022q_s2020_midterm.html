General Details

  • Date and time: Wed 8 April 2020, starting at 7:00pm
    • You need to turn up on time
    • If you arrive late you will not be able to take the exam and will receive zero marks
  • Duration: approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes long
  • Format: online canvas quiz
    • The canvas quiz can be accessed at https://canvas.ust.hk/courses/29889/quizzes/28520
    • It will only be accessible when the exam starts
    • You have to get ready at least 10 minutes before 7pm by logging in the canvas website and go to the COMP1022Q assignment page
    • A mock exam with a couple of questions will be given a week before the actual exam to help you know what you need to do during the real exam
  • The exam is open book and open notes

The Content of the Exam

  • The exam will have approximately 20 questions
  • You will work on the exam online inside a canvas quiz
  • Some questions are multiple-choice questions and some are short questions
    • For example, you could be shown a screen dump of a worksheet, and then asked to write the missing Excel formula
  • The exam could include questions about almost anything that has been discussed up to and including lecture 1 of week 7, and labs 1 to 4

Revising for The Exam

  • The exam is not really a test of memorisation, it is a test of understanding, so a good way to prepare is:
    • Go through all the notes and make sure you understand them
    • Go through all the labs and make sure you understand them
    • Download the example files and run all of them. Check you understand them!
