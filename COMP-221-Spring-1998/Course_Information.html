COMP 221 Fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence [3-1-0:3]

Course description
Foundations underlying design of intelligent systems. Relations between ogical, statistical, cognitive, biological paradigms; basic techniques for heuristic search, theorem proving, knowledge representation, adaptation; applications in vision, language, planning, expert systems.

Prerequisite
COMP171 (Data Structures and Algorithms)

Textbook
Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach, by S. Russell & P. Norvig, Prentice-Hall, 1995.

Requirements

    1. 5-6 Homework Assignments (30%)
    2. Exams (70%)
      Midterm Exam (30%)
      Final Exam (40%)
