Question 2:

Write a program that reads a 4-digit number, separates the number into its individual digits, and then prints the digits separated from one another by two spaces each.  For example, if the user types in 4402, the program should print

     4  4  0  2

(Hint: Use the integer division and modulo operators.)

Question 3:

Repeat the above, but the digits should be printed in reverse order.  For the input 4402, the output will be

      2  0  4  4

Medium difficulty

Question 4:

Write a C++ program to compute a water and sewer bill. The input is the number of gallons consumed. A water and sewer bill is computed as follows:

        - The water charge is .021 dollars per 100 gallons
        - The sewer charge is .001 dollars per 100 gallons
        - A service charge of 2 percent is applied to the total of the water and sewer charges

Your program's input and output should look like the following:

        Enter number of gallons of water consumed: 100000
    Water charge: $21
    Sewer charge: $1
    Service charge: $0.44
    Total charge: $22.44
