Course Information

    Course Descriptions

  • Advanced principles in computer and communication networking
  • Multimedia Networking;
  • Content Distribution over P2P Networks;
  • Wireless Networking;
  • IoT and Mobile Sensing;
  • Introduction to Network Security and Wireless Security;
  • Advanced Topics related to Congestion Control.
