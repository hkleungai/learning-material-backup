This page provides a quick explanation of assignment grading, answering the most common questions.

As  mentioned in class,  assignments are graded using a scale of 0-2.

A 2 denotes getting most of the assignment correct; 
a 1, some of the assignment correct; 
a 0, mostly wrong or not submitted.

There were many students who received a 1 for assignment 1, even though they wrote down correct numerical answers.  The reason for this was that the assignment specifically stated that

For all questions you should also provide a short explanation as to how you derived the solution. That is, if the solution is 20, you shouldn’t just write down 20. You need to explain why the answer is 20.

Students who did not provide any explanations were deemed to have answered the problems incorrectly and therefore received  a 1.
