Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 box of macaroni
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/4 cup of flour
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Cook the macaroni
  2. Mix the butter, flour and salt in a saucepan
  3. Add and stir the milk until thicken
  4. Add slowly the cheese until fully melt
  5. Mix with the macaroni
