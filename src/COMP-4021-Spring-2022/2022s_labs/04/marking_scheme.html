COMP4021 Internet Computing > Lab 4 - Marking Scheme

Part 1 - Drawing the Sprites (20 marks)

  • Drawing the player
    • The player shows at least the first sprite of the animation - 5 marks
    • The player shows the animation of the sprite sequence correctly, without looping when the player is idling - 5 marks
  • Drawing the gem
    • The gem shows at least the first sprite of the animation - 5 marks
    • The gem shows the animation of the sprite sequence correctly, with looping - 5 marks

Part 2 - Moving the Sprites (20 marks)

  • Moving the player
    • The player is moved appropriately when the arrow keys are pressed - 5 marks
    • The player is stopped appropriately when the arrow keys are released - 5 marks
  • Moving the gem
    • The gem is randomized at the start of the game - 5 marks
    • The gem is randomized every 3 seconds - 5 marks

Part 3 - Collecting the Gems (10 marks)

  • Collecting the gems
    • The player can collect the gems when the player is close to the gem - 5 marks
    • The gem is randomized after it is collected - 5 marks

Part 4 - Handling the Game Over Situation (15 marks)

  • Game over when the time is up
    • The timer stops at 0 second - 5 marks
    • The game over screen is shown appropriately - 5 marks
    • The number of gems collected is shown appropriately - 5 marks

Part 5 - Improvements (35 marks)

  • Adding Sounds
    • Some background music is played after starting the game - 5 marks
    • A collect sound effect is played when the player collects the gem each time - 5 marks
    • Some gameover music is played when the time is up - 5 marks
  • Adding Some Fires
    • Some fires/bombs are added to the four corners of the game - 10 marks
  • Adding a Cheat Key
    • Pressing the spacebar key makes the player move faster - 5 marks
    • Releasing the spacebar key makes the player move normally - 5 marks
