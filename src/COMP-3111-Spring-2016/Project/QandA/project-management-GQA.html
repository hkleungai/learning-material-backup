Individual Contribution

Q:

Regarding the individual contribution form, if there is a member of my team who did not contribute at all, would our whole team's total score be penalized if I report honestly about the free rider?

A:

The only score that will be penalized is that of the team member who is the free rider. That is the purpose of the individual contribution form—to prevent a team member from free-riding. If the other team members have 100% contribution, then they will receive the full score of the team for the activity. So, please be honest so that we can find out who are the free-riders. Otherwise we have no way of knowing.


Minutes

Q:

If the minutes are not approved in the formal project meeting as an accurate record, should I hand in the unapproved minutes?

A:

Yes. Obviously you cannot approve the minutes until the next meeting which will be after the weekly deadline for submitting the minutes for the previous week.

Q:

If a meeting is adjourned and then resumed the next day, should I title the minutes as "Minutes of the resumed 2nd scrum meeting" instead of the "Minutes of the 3rd scrum meeting"?

A:

Sure, but a meeting should not be so long that it needs to be adjourned until the next day!
