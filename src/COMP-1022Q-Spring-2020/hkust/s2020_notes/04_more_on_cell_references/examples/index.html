COMP1022Q Introduction to Computing with Excel VBA Notes Examples - More on Cell References

  • 01_1022q_range_references.xlsx [from page 4 of the notes]
    • This example demonstrates different kinds of range referencing, which refer to a range of cells in formulas, e.g. one cell, several cells, several consecutive cells, rows, columns, etc.
  • 02_1022q_iphone_profit_wrong_formula.xlsx [from page 17 of the notes]
    • This example demonstrates that when absolute and relative referencing are not used correctly, results will become completely wrong
    • The purpose of the worksheet is to calculate the profit of flipping (buying and then reselling quickly) iPhone 6 in different countries
  • 03_1022q_iphone_profit_correct_formula.xlsx [from page 18 of the notes]
    • This example uses absolute and relative referencing at appropriate places in the formula so that the results are correctly computed
    • The purpose of the worksheet is to calculate the profit of flipping (buying and then reselling quickly) iPhone 6 in different countries
