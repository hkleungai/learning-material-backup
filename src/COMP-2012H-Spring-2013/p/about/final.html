COMP 152H Final Exam Grade Appeal Procedure

  • You have a chance to read the graded final. This is optional and you don't have to do it. But if you do, the schedule is as follows:

    Date: May 25, 2011
    Time: 3:30pm - 4:30pm
    Venue: 4209 (TA's office)

    The graded final must return to the TA-in-charge.

  • If you cannot make it, study the final exam solution carefully. In some cases, you are advised to type in your solution, compile and run. If it does not run, don't waste your time. Partial credits were often granted already in many of these cases.

    If you decide to appeal:

  • Send an email to chiumk@cs (cc to cktang@cs), to
    point out which part of the question is problematic (e.g. 1(e))
    explain briefly why you deserve more (or less) points. All final exam grade appeal must be made before 9:00 June 3, 2011. Late appeals will not be considered. This is a strict deadline.

  • One of us will re-grade this question which may result in increase or decrease in your total score.

  • After re-grading, your email will be replied.
