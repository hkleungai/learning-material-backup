COMP104: Programming

Week 5 : Extra Programming Exercise (not marked or handed in): Crossing the Bridge


Medium difficulty

In Extra 5, you can implement the game Crossing the Bridge. At the start of the game, there is a family of 5 persons on one side of a bridge. It is at night and there is only 1 lamp. Your task is to help the family to cross the bridge before the lamp uses up the fuel, which can last for 30 minutes only. A maximum of 2 persons can cross at one time, and they must have the lamp with them. Each person walks at a different speed: 1 min, 3 min, 6 min, 8 min and 12 min. A pair must walk together at the same speed of the slower person. You can try the game HERE!
