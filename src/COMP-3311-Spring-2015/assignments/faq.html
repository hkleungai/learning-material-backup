Frequently Asked Questions

Q1:

In Assignment 1a(ER diagram), the second point of the description of the problem says that "The courses are offered at most once in each semester", does this means that each course (e.g COMP3311) only have one section (e.g L1 only) in each semester?

A1:

No, it does not mean that each course only have one section. It means that to uniquely identify a course offering, you just need the year, semester and section number (e.g L1) of the course offering together with the primary key, course ID, of its owner entity set, course. For comparison, think about that, if one course can be offered twice in a semester, for example, once half of one semester, then to uniquely identify a course offering, we need additional information about in which half of the semester this course is offered.

The relationship between course and course offering here is very similar to that of the first exercise in Tutorial 1 (ER diagrams), you can also refer to the tutorial slide for help.
