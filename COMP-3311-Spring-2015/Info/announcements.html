Academic integrity

Unless otherwise stated, the work that you submit for grading must represent the result of your own independent effort. In particular, you are not allowed to give solutions to other students or to copy solutions from other students (past or present). Suspected cheating will be referred to the relevant university authorities for disciplinary action.
If in doubt, consult with the instructor or TA first!
