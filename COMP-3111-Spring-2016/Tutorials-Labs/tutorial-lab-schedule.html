COMP 3111: Software Engineering

Tutorial and Lab Schedule

IMPORTANT NOTE

Most labs will be used to implement some of the course project requirements. Labs used for this purpose will count toward the course Lab grade. What you do in a lab will be based on the material covered during the tutorial. Therefore, it is important that you attend each week's tutorial so that you know what you will need to do in the lab that week. There will be no time to cover the tutorial material during the lab period.
The credit for a lab consists of (1) the activity that you do during the lab session and (2) the assigned homework.
