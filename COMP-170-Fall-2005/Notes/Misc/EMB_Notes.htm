More on Ciphers

In Spring 2005, Professor Cunsheng Ding and myself gave a short course for advanced secondary school students on Decrypting Historical Ciphers. The slides for this course give more information on substitution ciphers and how to break them, and also on the RSA public-key encryption system.

Those slides, with further links, are available here.

Note that none of this extra material is being taught in class and it is not considered part of the class material, i.e., it will not appear on any exam.
