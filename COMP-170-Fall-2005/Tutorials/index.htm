Supplementary Advanced Classes

In addition to the supplementary review session (in which questions about class material will be answered) I will also be giving Supplementary Advanced Classes.  These sessions are meant to teach extra, more advanced, topics that can not be fitted into our syllabus but might be of interest to some students.  Attendance at these advanced classes are totally optional and new material taught in these advanced classes WILL NOT be on any class assignment or exam. 

Giving these advanced classes is an experiment.  If students are interested, they will continue to be offered all semester.  If students are not interested,  they will be dropped. Here is the planned schedule for these advanced classes (note that they mostly alternate weeks with the supplementary review sessions):

  1. Tuesday, September 20, 2005,  18-18:50, Lecture Theatre F (LTF)
  2. Tuesday, October 18, 2005,  18-18:50, Lecture Theatre F (LTF)
  3. Tuesday, November 15, 2005,  18-18:50, Lecture Theatre F (LTF)
