Videos for Self-Study

The videos of an earlier year with a slower paced introduction covering this material will also be made available and may be referenced by the instructors for further emphasis on some topics. Students who have more limited programming experience or prefer a slower paced introduction of the basic programming concepts again are encoraged to also see these videos.
