Midterm Grade Appeal Procedure

  • Please study the midterm solution carefully. You are advised not to quibble over one or two "items" worthing less than 1 point (out of 35). Honestly, it doesn't make any difference.
  • In programming problems, you are advised to type in your solution, compile and run. If it does not run, don't waste your time.
    Partial credits were often granted already in many of these cases.
  • All midterm grade appeal must be made before April 11, 2013 (Thursday). Late appeals will not be considered. This is a strict deadline.

    If you decide to appeal:

  • Give the exam to Dr. Tang directly.
  • Send an email to cktang@cs, to
    point out which part of the question is problematic (e.g. 1(e))
    explain briefly why you deserve more (or less) points.
  • Dr. Tang will re-grade this question which may result in increase or decrease in your total score. After re-grading, your email will be replied. The re-graded exam will be returned to you.
