Since this is the first assignment of the semester we are providing some extra clarification.

 

  • The assignments should be submitted by 5pm of the due date in the assignment collection bin in the corridor in front of room 4213A. Solutions will be available the day after submissions are due, so late submissions will not be accepted.
     
  • Please submit a hardcopy of your assignment (emailed solutions will not be accepted).
    Either printed or handwritten hardcopies will be accepted.  If writing by hand,  please do not write too small.
     
  • Keep a copy of your submitted solution around.  If we can't find your submission,  we might ask you for the copy.
     
  • Some of the questions require material NOT taught during the first class that will be taught on Tuesday, 4 September. To get this material you can either read the last two pages of section 1.1 in the textbook or check out the end of the Lecture 1 notes.
     
  • The notation

which is read as  n choose 2, denotes the value n(n-1)/2.  Some of you might have previously seen this written as nC2.  Please do not use the nC2 notation in any of your assignment answers.  This is an older notation which has been disappearing (we will see later in class why it sometimes has some problems).

  • Some students have asked what is meant by "providing  a short explanation as to how you derived the solution".  For examples,  please see the model solutions  given for this Model Assignment.
     

  • Hint for Problem 5. Consider your  solution to Problem 4.  There are actually two different ways to attempt  to solve problem 4; the first corresponds to  first choosing the president and then choosing the executive board;  the second corresponds to first choosing the executive board and then choosing the president.  The two ways of thinking will lead to two different formulas.  But, since they describe the same thing, the two formulas must be equal.  Now try and relate this to Problem 5.
