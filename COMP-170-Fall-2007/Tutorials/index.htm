Supplementary Advanced Classes

In addition to the supplementary review session (in which questions about class material will be answered)  we will also be giving Supplementary Advanced Classes.  These sessions are meant to teach extra, more advanced, topics that can not be fitted into our syllabus but might be of interest to some students.  Attendance at these advanced classes are totally optional and new material taught in these advanced classes WILL NOT be on any class assignment or exam.  The two instructors will be presenting almost the same material in their sessions and you are welcome to attend either instructor's session, depending upon your schedule.
