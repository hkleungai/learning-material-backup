COMP4021 Internet Computing > Lab 2 - Marking Scheme

Part 1 - The Countdown Screen (15 marks)

  • The count down timer
    • The countdown timer starts at 3 - 5 marks
    • The countdown timer changes from 3 to 2, 2 to 1 and 1 to "Start" in 1 second interval - 10 marks

Part 2 - Starting the Game (20 marks)

  • Hiding the countdown screen
    • The countdown screen is hidden after the game starts - 5 marks
    • Some form of a short animation (fade/slide) is used when the countdown screen is hidden - 5 marks
  • The game area
    • The 9 holes are visible in the game area - 5 marks
    • The 9 holes are arranged in 3 rows of 3 columns in the center of the screen - 5 marks

Part 3 - Showing the Monsters (40 marks)

  • Showing and hiding the monster
    • The monster first appears in a hole two seconds after the start of the game - 10 marks
    • The monster then disappears two seconds after it is shown - 5 marks
    • The monster keeps on showing/disappearing in random holes - 5 marks
    • When the monster is visible, it fits inside a hole - 5 marks
  • Clicking on the monster
    • When the monster is clicked on, it disappears immediately - 10 marks
    • After clicking on the monster a few times, the monster still appears and disappears in a reasonable time interval - 5 marks

Part 4 - Game Over (25 marks)

  • The hole border
    • When the player fails to kill the monster once, the hole border turns to yellow - 5 marks
    • When the player fails to kill the monster again, the hole border turns to red - 5 marks
  • Game over screen
    • When the player fails to kill the monster the third time, the game over screen is shown - 5 marks
    • Some form of a short animation (fade/slide) is used when the game over screen is shown - 5 marks
  • The game difficulty
    • The game gets more difficult, i.e. the monster appears and disappears in a shorter time, every time the player kills the monster - 5 marks
